What is WeCBR?

WeCBR is a Student Help Club. CBR basically means Community Based Rehabilitation at student level.

We know that this is the era of Information, it means information is available free of cost.
WeCBR aims to help students, who have faced lack of awareness in the initial days of colleges by providing them information
about various events, internship offers and resources that makes them independent and aware.
